How to Earn the Badge

Three steps to become a recognized Cloud Native Valencia speaker

1

Submit Your Talk

Submit a talk proposal through our CFP on Sessionize. We accept sessions on Kubernetes, cloud-native technologies, DevOps, SRE, platform engineering, and related topics.

2

Get Accepted

The event organizers review all submissions and notify accepted speakers. Accepted talks are chosen based on relevance, quality, and community value.

3

Present at the Event

Deliver your session at an official Cloud Native Valencia meetup. After the event, you'll receive your digital speaker badge to share and display proudly.
